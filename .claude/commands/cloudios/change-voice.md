# Change your TTS voice

Changes the text-to-speech voice for your CLOUDIOS agent.

Usage: `/cloudios:change-voice <agent-name> <voice-name>`

Example: `/cloudios:change-voice herman-cloudios Samantha`

To see available voices, use: `/cloudios:voices-list`
