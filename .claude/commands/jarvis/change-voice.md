# Change your TTS voice

Changes the text-to-speech voice for your JARVIS agent.

Usage: `/jarvis:change-voice <agent-name> <voice-name>`

Example: `/jarvis:change-voice herman-jarvis Samantha`

To see available voices, use: `/jarvis:voices-list`
