ExpectedLifeNThe expected life in years of the asset
CostNThe cost (per unit) of the asset
AccumDepreciationNThe accumulated depreciation recorded against the asset
AcquisitionDateDDate on which the asset was acquired
LastDepreciatedDateDDate on which the asset was last depreciated
AcquisitionSeqNThe sequencenumber of the purchase transaction for the asset
DisposalSeqNThe sequencenumber of the last disposal transaction for the asset

StatusT3The asset status (NEW, ACT - active, NDP - non-depreciable, OTH - other, DSP - disposed)

Assetlog:

Associated with each asset is the assetlog subfile, which is a log of each action (depreciation, revaluations etc) in the life of the asset.
