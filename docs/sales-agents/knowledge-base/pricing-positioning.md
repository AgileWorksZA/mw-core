# MoneyWorks Pricing Strategy & Sales Positioning

## Quick Reference Pricing (South African Rand)

### Product Tiers Overview
1. **Cashbook** - FREE (Basic accounting)
2. **Express** - R370/month or R6,800 perpetual
3. **Gold** - R590/month or R13,500 perpetual  
4. **Datacentre** - R1,050/month or R45,000 perpetual
5. **Now** - R890/month (Cloud-only, no perpetual)

## The MoneyWorks Pricing Pitch (FOGG-Optimized)

### Initial Response to "What does it cost?"

**Primary Response (Value-First):**
"MoneyWorks offers several options to match your business needs, from R370 to R1,050 per month. But before I quote specific pricing, let me ask - what's currently costing you more: the hours spent on manual accounting processes, or the opportunities missed due to poor financial visibility?"

**Alternative Response (Growth-Focused):**
"We have solutions ranging from free for basic needs up to enterprise-level at R1,050 monthly. Most growing businesses find our Gold tier at R590/month delivers the best value. What specific capabilities are most important for your business?"

**Alternative Response (ROI-Focused):**
"MoneyWorks typically pays for itself within 3-6 months through efficiency gains alone. We have options from R370 monthly, but the real question is: what's the monthly cost of NOT having real-time financial insights?"

## FOGG-Based Pricing Conversation Framework

### 1. Reframe from Cost to Investment (Motivation)
**Pain-Cost Connection:**
- "At R590/month for Gold, that's less than 4 hours of a bookkeeper's time"
- "Compare R1,050/month to the cost of one financial reporting error"
- "Most clients save 20+ hours monthly - what's that worth to your business?"

**Opportunity-Value Connection:**
- "The ability to see real-time profitability across locations is worth far more than R1,050/month"
- "One better business decision based on accurate data pays for MoneyWorks for years"

### 2. Make Investment Feel Manageable (Ability)
**Payment Flexibility:**
- "We offer both monthly subscriptions and one-time perpetual licenses"
- "Start with Express at R370/month and upgrade as you grow"
- "No long-term contracts - pay monthly and adjust as needed"

**Gradual Adoption:**
- "Many clients start with Gold and add Datacentre when they need multi-user access"
- "You can begin with core accounting and add modules over time"

### 3. Create Urgency Around Current Costs (Trigger)
**Hidden Cost Awareness:**
- "How many hours per month does your team spend on manual data entry?"
- "What decisions are you delaying because you don't have current financial data?"
- "Every month you wait costs you in efficiency and missed opportunities"

## Objection Handling: Price Concerns

### "That's more than we're paying now"
**Response:** "You're right, MoneyWorks might cost more than basic bookkeeping software. Let me ask - what's the monthly cost of the inefficiencies in your current system? Most clients find they save more in staff time than MoneyWorks costs."

### "We need to compare prices"
**Response:** "Absolutely, you should compare. When you do, look beyond the sticker price. Compare the total cost including add-ons, training, and most importantly - the cost of what each system CAN'T do that you need."

### "Can you discount the price?"
**Response:** "Rather than discounting, let's ensure you're getting maximum value. Would you prefer to start with a lower tier and upgrade later, or would the perpetual license option work better for your budget?"

### "It's not in our budget"
**Response:** "I understand budgets are tight. Many clients fund MoneyWorks from the savings it generates. If we could show you how to save 20 hours monthly, would that change the budget conversation?"

## ROI Calculation Framework

**Standard ROI Points:**
- Staff time savings: 20-40 hours/month @ R150/hour = R3,000-6,000
- Error reduction: 1-2 prevented errors/month @ R2,000 each = R2,000-4,000
- Faster decisions: 1 week faster insights = immeasurable value
- Typical payback: 3-6 months
