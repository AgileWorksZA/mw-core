- Show successful implementations in similar situations
- Demonstrate ROI and business impact
- Provide evidence of smooth transitions

### Follow-up Materials
- Offer detailed case studies for qualified prospects
- Provide reference customer contact information
- Share implementation timeline examples
- Include ROI calculation templates
