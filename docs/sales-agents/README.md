# MoneyWorks AI Sales Agents Project

## Overview

This project develops a series of AI agents specifically designed to sell the MoneyWorks accounting software suite. The agents are built using advanced behavioral research principles (FOGG Behavioral Model) and leverage our semantic canonical ontology to understand MoneyWorks capabilities deeply.

## Development Approach

### 1. Research Foundation
- **Market Analysis**: Deep dive into accounting software market segments
- **Behavioral Research**: Apply FOGG Behavioral Model for persuasion
- **Pain Point Mapping**: Identify specific business challenges MoneyWorks solves

### 2. Semantic Ontology Integration
- Leverage our 99.6% coverage semantic ontology
- Map MoneyWorks capabilities to business outcomes
- Create natural language understanding of complex features

### 3. Agent Development
- **Lead Qualification Agent**: Primary entry point for prospects
- **Technical Specialist**: Deep technical discussions
- **Industry Specialists**: Tailored for specific verticals

### 4. Knowledge Base
- Comprehensive MoneyWorks capability database
- Competitive positioning information
- Success stories and case studies
- Pricing and implementation guidance

## Target Markets

### Primary Segments
1. **Growing SMEs** (20-200 employees)
2. **Multi-location businesses** requiring consolidated reporting
3. **Service-based companies** needing project/job costing
4. **Distributors/Wholesalers** requiring inventory management
5. **Professional services** needing time tracking and billing

### Secondary Segments
1. **Non-profits** requiring fund accounting
2. **Educational institutions** 
3. **Government agencies** needing compliance reporting
4. **International businesses** requiring multi-currency support

## Success Metrics

- **Lead Qualification Rate**: Percentage of contacts qualifying for sales engagement
- **Conversion Rate**: Qualified leads to sales opportunities
- **Time to Qualification**: Average time to determine lead quality
- **Agent Satisfaction**: Prospect feedback on interaction quality
- **Sales Team Efficiency**: Improvement in sales productivity
