# Purchase Enquiry

## Overview
Purchase Enquiry provides drill-down analysis of supplier purchases, payment history, and stock procurement.

## Access Points
- Right sidebar: Purchase Enquiry icon
- Enquiries menu: Payments History (⌥⇧⌘H), Stock Enquiry (⌘9)

## Enquiry Types

### Payments History (⌥⇧⌘H)
- View payment history for a supplier
- Track payment patterns and timing

### Stock Enquiry (⌘9)
- View stock levels and movements
- Purchase history per item
- Supplier comparison for items

## Common Features
- Supplier/item selection
- Date range filtering
- Period comparison
- Drill-down to source transactions
