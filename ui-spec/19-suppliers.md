# Suppliers

## Overview
Suppliers share the same Names register as Customers (see [18-customers.md](18-customers.md) for full specification). A Name record with the "Supplier" checkbox enabled appears in supplier-filtered views.

## Access Points
- Right sidebar: Suppliers icon
- Names list sidebar: Suppliers → Cash-only Suppliers, Creditors (Credit Suppliers)

## Supplier-Specific Features
- **Creditor flag**: Enables credit purchasing and payables tracking
- **Creditor Control** (Pricing & Terms tab): Payment terms, Payables Control account, Payment Method
- **Bank and EDI tab**: Electronic banking details for outgoing payments
- **WE OWE** balance on Balances tab

## Supplier-Specific Reports (from Names sidebar)
- Aged Payables
- Item by Supplier
- Purchases over Time

## Notes
The Name record is unified — the same record serves as both Customer and Supplier when both checkboxes are enabled. See [18-customers.md](18-customers.md) for the complete form specification.
